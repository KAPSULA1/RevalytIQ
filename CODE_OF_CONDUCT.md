# Code of Conduct

We adopt the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) to foster an open, welcoming environment.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in RevalytIQ a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behaviour:

- Demonstrating empathy and respect.
- Providing constructive feedback.
- Accepting responsibility and apologising to those affected by mistakes.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Use of sexualised language or imagery.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Report violations to **conduct@example.com**. Project maintainers will review and respond within a reasonable timeframe. All complaints will be kept confidential.

## Enforcement Guidelines

1. **Correction:** Maintainers may issue a private, written warning.
2. **Warning:** Continued behaviour may result in a warning with consequences.
3. **Temporary Ban:** Temporary suspension from the project.
4. **Permanent Ban:** Removal from the community for severe violations.
